# Bran muffins

### Makes

12

## Ingredients

* 2 1/4 c bran
* 1 1/4 c flour
* 1 tsp baking powder
* 1/3 c sugar
* pinch salt

* 1 c milk
* 1 tsp baking soda

* 60g butter
* 75g agave or maple or sugar syrup

* 1/2 c fruit (raisins, berries)

## Method

Oven to 200 C / 390 F.

Mix dry with wet, add fruit.

Bake ~20 mins.
