# Bran muffins

### Makes

12

## Ingredients

Dry:

* 2 1/4 c bran (135g)
* 1 1/4 c flour (166g)
* 1/3 c sugar (75g)
* 1 tsp baking powder 
* pinch salt

Mix:

* 1 c milk (245g)
* 1 tsp baking soda

Microwave:

* 60g butter
* 50g sugar syrup
* 25g agave

Add:

* 1 c fruit (raisins, berries)

## Method

Oven to 200 C / 390 F.

Mix dry with wet, add fruit.

Bake ~20 mins.
