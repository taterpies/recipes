# Gypsy Soup

### Source

Moosewood cookbook

### Makes

4x double portions.

## Ingredients and method.

* 1 large japanese sweet potato
* 2 sticks celery
* 1 onion

Dice and saute.

* 3 cloves garlic crushed
* 1 red pepper diced
* 2 tsp salt

Add and cook 2 mins.

* 2 tsp paprika
* 1 tsp tumeric
* 2 tsp dried basil
* 1 tsp dried chilli flakes
* 1/4 tsp cinnamon
* 2 large bay leaves

Add and fry 1 minute.

* 14oz can chickpeas
* 14oz can crushed tomatoes
* 2 qt stock

Add and simmer.
