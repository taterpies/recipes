# Carrot Pie

### Makes

To fill large roasting pan.

## Ingredients

* 2 1/2 c white flour
* 1/2 c wholemeal flour
* 170g butter
* 100g ice water
* 1/2 tsp baking powder
* 1 tsp salt
* 1 tsp mustard

* 2 1/2 kg (5 lbs) carrots
* 1/3 c cider vinegar
* 1 c grated cheddar

## Method

Oven to 360 F.

Make pastry. Pre bake.

Dice and boil carrots. Combine to make filling.

Bake 25 mins.
