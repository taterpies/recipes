# Carrot Pie

### Makes

To fill lasagne dish

## Ingredients

### Pastry

Serious eats tender whole-wheat recipe

* 4 oz white flour
* 4 oz whole-wheat flour
* 6 tbs butter
* 5 oz ice water
* 1/2 tsp baking powder
* 1 tsp salt

* 1 kg diced carrot
* 3 tbs cider vin
* 3/4 c grated cheddar
* black pepper

## Method

Blind bake pastry 30 mins @ 350F.

Bake pie 30 mins @ 350 + 3 mins broil for top.
