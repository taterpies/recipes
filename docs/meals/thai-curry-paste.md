# Thai curry pastes

## Source

[Thompson](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC1ZlulquuoTtzqVRcwf79Ow)

## Massaman

### Makes

5 cubes

## Ingredients and method

toast:

* 25g garlic
* 90g shallots
* 4 lemongrass
* 35g ginger

add:

* dried peppers (2 arbol 6 pulla)

toast, then grind:

* 2 tsp coriander seed
* 1/4 tsp cumin seed
* 1/4 tsp cardamom
* 1/8 tsp cinnamon
* 3x cloves
* 1/4 tsp nutmeg/mace

## Green

### Makes

6 cubes

## Ingredients

* 4x lemongrass (18g total)
* 1"^2 lime peel
* 6 birdseye peppers, deseeded (~11g whole, 6g prepped)
* 1/2 tsp coarse salt for grinding
* 20g galangal
* 20g ginger
* 7 cl large garlic (24g peeled)
* 5 shallots (105g peeled)
* 1" tumeric (8g)
* 1/2 tsp cumin seed
* 1/2 tsp white pepper
* 1/2 tsp coriander seed
