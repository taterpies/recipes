# Thai curry pastes

## Source

https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC1ZlulquuoTtzqVRcwf79Ow

## Massaman

### Makes

6 cubes

## Ingredients and method

* dried peppers (2 arbol 6 pulla)
* 25g garlic
* 90g shallots
* 4 lemongrass
* 35g ginger

toast, then grind

* 2 tsp coriander seed
1/4 tsp cumin seed
1/4 tsp cardamom
1/8 tsp cinnamon
3x cloves
1/4 tsp nutmeg/mace
