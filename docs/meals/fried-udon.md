# Spicy Udon Stir Fry

## Source

[Link](https://www.seasonsandsuppers.ca/15-minute-spicy-udon-vegetable-stir-fry/)

## Mods

Add tofu, greens, 1 birdseye pepper.

16 oz (450g) pack of udon noodles.

Sauce:

* 2 tsp sambal olek
* 2 tsp rice wine vin
* 2 tbs tamari
* 2 tbs kecap manis
* 1 tbs agave
* 1 tsp sesame oil
* 2 cl garlic
* 1/4" ginger
