# Roti canai

### Source

[Link](http://ieatishootipost.sg/how-to-make-roti-prata-aka-roti-canai-everything-you-need-to-know/)

### Makes

5

## Ingredients

* 300g flour
* 150ml water
* 1 egg
* 1/2 tsp salt
* 1 tsp sugar
* 1/2 tbs oil

## Method

* Process 20s
* Rest 20m
* Process 30s
* Rest 2m
* Process 30s

Divide and shape, rest 1-12 hours, cook in hot pan.
