# Tomato relish

### Source

[Link](http://www.bite.co.nz/recipe/16381/Nanas-tomato-relish/)

### Makes

1 500ml jar

## Ingredients

* 500g tomatoes
* 3/4 tsp salt

* 1/2 onion
* 1/2 c sugar
* 1/2 c malt vinegar
* 1 tbs lemon guice
* 1/4 tsp curry powder
* 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
* 1 birdseye chilli pepper
* 1/4 c raisins

## Method

Salt tomatoes, leave for hours.

Boil all. Add sugar, boil 1 min and jar.
